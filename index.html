Ognian's Music Project

This project explores the interaction between classical melodies and various digital techniques and musical styles within Logic Pro. Thanks for listening!


Claude Debussy - Arabesque No. 1

This experiment involves transforming Debussy's famous Arabesuqe into a melodic rock guitar quartet with supplementary rock drums.

Brahms - Hungarian Dance No. 1

This experiment involves semi-orchestrating Brahms' Hungarian Dance No. 1 using a variety of classical and non-classical instruments that were not part of the original composition.

Ottorino Respighi - Six Pieces for Piano

This experiment involved layering Respighi's first piano piece with drums, guitars, and other instruments to create a jazzy piece.

Franz Liszt - Totentanz

This experiment is the transformations of Liszt's piano composition Totentanz into a DnB (Drum n Bass) song. Unfortunately, this piece isn't finished due to me having to be hospitalized on the 13th of December.

Guiseppe Verdi - Rigoletto

This experiment involved creating an abridged version of the "La donna è mobile" aria from Verdi's Rigoletto. The style I was going for is the one used in Stanley Kubrick's Clockwork Orange.

Sergei Rachmaninoff - Symphony 2

This experiment involved transofrming the third movement of Rachmaninoff's 2nd Symphony into a jazz/blues composition. The piece is abridged.

Franz Schubert - Ständchen

Originally this piece was written for piano and voice. In this experiment I orchestrate it for piano, choir, and various instruments through Logic Pro.
